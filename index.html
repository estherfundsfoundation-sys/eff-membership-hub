YOUR PATH INTO EFF

Not just a membership.
A preparation.

Every candidate sees the next right step. Progress is personal, requirements are clear, and official membership is earned through purpose-filled preparation.

  1. 01Accepted by EFFYour chapter/national process confirms your place in The Esther Experience.
  2. 02Join ItComplete your free national registration through the official EFF roster.
  3. 03Esther ExperienceLearn, serve, connect, reflect, and complete your candidate requirements.
  4. 04ClearanceFinish your checklist and receive clearance for induction.
  5. 05Official memberAttend induction and unlock your full EFF member life.
